Emperor Tewodros of Ethiopia

Tewodros II was Emperor of Ethiopia from 1855 until his death in 1868. He is known for bringing the end to rule of princes which was called the Zemene Mesafint. During Zemene Mesafint Ethipiopia experienced decentralized governing system. Emperor brought an end to decentralized giving system and introduced the beginning of united and modern Ethiopia. Though he was not from the nobility blood line, but he made to the throne in 1855 and became Tewodros II of Ethiopia. He ruled Ethiopia from 1855-1868.
